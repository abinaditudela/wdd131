Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best housing for rabbits?

Rabbits need a clean, dry, and safe environment. A hutch or cage with plenty of space, proper ventilation, and secure locks is ideal.

What should I feed my rabbits?

A balanced diet for rabbits includes hay, fresh vegetables, and a small amount of pellets. Fresh water should always be available.

How do I care for a sick rabbit?

If your rabbit appears sick, it's important to consult a veterinarian who specializes in rabbits. Keep the rabbit warm, hydrated, and isolated from other pets.

How often should rabbits be bred?

Rabbits can be bred throughout the year, but it's recommended to give female rabbits a break between litters to ensure their health and well-being.

What is the lifespan of a rabbit?

Rabbits typically live between 8 to 12 years, depending on their breed, care, and living conditions.
