Rabbit Breeding

Responsible breeding practices for healthy kits

Key Aspects of Rabbit Breeding

Breeding Age

Females can start breeding at 5-6 months, males at 6-7 months.

Gestation

Rabbit gestation lasts approximately 30 days.

Nesting

Provide a nesting box a few days before birth with soft materials like straw or hay.

Litter Size

Litters typically consist of 4 to 12 kits.

Breeding Tips

  • Allow mothers to nurse for 3-4 weeks
  • Gradually introduce solid food to kits from 3 weeks
  • Give female rabbits rest periods between pregnancies
  • Keep detailed records of breeding pairs and litters
