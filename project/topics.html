Properties of Rabbit Meat

Rabbit meat is exquisite and accepts all types of cooking methods: baked, roasted, grilled, fried, boiled, stewed, in sausages, etc. Due to the inherent characteristics of rabbits, we can present rabbit meat as the healthiest on the market. It is entirely white meat, meaning it has no red meat cuts and other white ones.

Being white meat, all chicken recipes adapt perfectly to it. It is a very homogeneous meat, as all its cuts are very tender and lean. This means there is no significant difference in quality between one cut and another. It's worth remembering that rabbits do not admit any type of growth promoter, such as hormones, which are eventually assimilated by humans causing endocrine problems such as; precocious puberty, thyroiditis and overweight, among others.

Nutritional Benefits

  • High in protein that's easily assimilated by the human body
  • Rich in iron, potassium, calcium, and vitamins (particularly B complex)
  • Very low in sodium and uric acid

For all these reasons, this meat is ideal for: babies, pregnant and lactating women, older adults, people with digestive problems, high cholesterol, heart issues or hypertension, and in general, for people who wish to maintain or improve their health or figure, without sacrificing flavor and quality.
