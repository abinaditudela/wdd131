Rabbit Care

Ensuring the health and happiness of your rabbits

Essential Aspects of Rabbit Care

Health Checks

Perform regular health checks, including teeth, nails, and fur condition.

Exercise

Provide daily exercise opportunities outside the cage in a safe, rabbit-proofed area.

Grooming

Brush your rabbit regularly, especially during shedding seasons.

Veterinary Care

Schedule regular check-ups with a rabbit-savvy veterinarian.

Care Tips

  • Trim nails every 6-8 weeks
  • Provide chew toys for dental health
  • Keep the environment stress-free and quiet
  • Socialize with your rabbit daily
