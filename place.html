Cochabamba - My Favorite Place

INFORMATION

Cochabamba is known as the "City of Eternal Spring" for its pleasant climate and the "Garden City" for its lush vegetation. At 2,558 meters (8,392 feet) above sea level.

Cochabamba features the Cristo de la Concordia statue with panoramic views. It is also a key transportation and commerce hub in Bolivia.
